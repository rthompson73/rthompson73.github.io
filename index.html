Welcome to A Forever Home Animal Rescue, Inc. 501(3)(c) non-profit organization saving lives throughout Florida.


At AFHAR we give homeless dogs a place to be loved, cared for, trained and hope for a home. Our average intake is about 20 dogs per month coming
in from shelters, as strays, or owners who can no longer care for their pet. To find our dogs for adoption click here. We have several cats for adoption as well.
Please go to www.petfinder.com or www.rescueme.org for a full list of all pets for adoption.

AFHAR is looking for a piece of property to make our dream come true of moving and building our own facility.

If you are interested in boarding your dog, please call for an appointment. All proceeds are used to help rescued dogs.

Volunteering

AFHAR always needs people to volunteer for us. The rescue is growing and we are saving more dogs than ever. The following is a list of volunteer tasks needed. You may pick whichever task(s) fit your schedule or is what you prefer doing:
  • Working at our adoption center. We need people to come in to our center anytime during the hours of 7:30am-7:30pm to help with general cleaning, kennel work, walking dogs, etc.
  • Helping out at adoption events
  • Transporting dogs to and from the veterinary office. The vet we use the most is Piedmont Animal Hospital in Apopka.
  • Making phone calls : follow-up calls to adopters, calls to screen applicants for adoption, setting up vet transports
  • Fundraising
  • Fostering a dog in your home - a separate application process is required.
  • Bring your talents! Any way you can help our rescue would be appreciated
